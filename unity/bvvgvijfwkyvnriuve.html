We hear the footsteps of the people
The coins strike the ground above our lair
Still we know that we must stay here
Until our Lord arises from the deep

The merchants wish for peace
The soldiers wish for war
We can hear their heart's desires
Destiny will judge them all

Our wish is far more ancient
And soon it shall be fulfilled
The sounds of their wishes will be drowned out
By the deafening sound of silence.
